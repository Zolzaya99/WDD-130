Purpose

The purpose of this website is to introduce and broaden my private business. On my website, I will display my photography work, the different types of photoshoot I do, the price range and booking options. Reviewing my website would help the audience be provided with the basic information they need.

Audience

My target audience would be young adults because I mainly focus on engagements, wedding, graduation, couple photoshoots. I want my website to look modern, and I want my website colors to be pastel and warm pallette since mostly young adults would bereviewing my website.

Portfolio

[Here you can see my personal portfolio. I have a variety of images for you to look at and see what style you like the most. Based on the style or multiple styles you choose from my portfolio, we can come up with a plan and price range based on those calculations. Although I have specific styles and choices, I am open to trying new things and experimenting with new ideas in order to meet your photography needs. When selecting a photoshoot style from my portfolio, it is important to keep in mind that with each choice you make, you must choose a date and time that corresponds with my schedule. For example, if you choose to do a wedding photo shoot on your special day and someone else has already picked that day for me to shoot their wedding, I can not adjust that day to fit your schedule. So it is important to always remember when you are free and how that lines up with when I'm available.]
